The New Pillar

Why Al-Marj City?

Al-Rakiza Al-Jadida operates from a clear strategic vision aimed at bridging the gap between vast pastoral potential and available services, with Al-Marj as the launchpad and hub for its integrated veterinary services.

Strategic Location

Heart of Seasonal Migration

Al-Marj is one of the richest regions in livestock wealth, yet it suffers from a severe shortage of advanced veterinary services.

Al-Marj represents Libya's largest livestock migration corridor for most of the year (March–October), requiring proactive veterinary intervention to protect national food security.

1
Field Care

Reaching the Breeder System

We don't wait for cases to come to us — our specialized mobile veterinary teams reach breeders in their fields and farms for regular follow-ups and immediate treatment.

Our staff are qualified to handle large animals (ruminants), poultry, and companion animals — comprehensive service with no exceptions.

2
Advanced Diagnostics

Technology & Our Future Vision

A fully integrated veterinary laboratory in Al-Marj city for early disease detection and accurate analyses as quickly as possible.

Advanced imaging services: X-Ray and Ultrasound within a region that previously had none.

3
International Partnerships

Knowledge Transfer & Global Standards

We host international experts and scientific visits from top global companies with which we have formed strategic partnerships, to elevate the level of local staff.

We import the finest veterinary medicines and equipment according to the highest international standards, keeping pace with the latest scientific advances in poultry, livestock, and companion animal treatment.

4

We Are Here Because Al-Marj Deserves the Best

At "Al-Rakiza Al-Jadida" we strive to transform the region from an underserved area into a model for comprehensive veterinary care at the national level.
